The Greatest Story Never Told

Like most kids in elementary school, I once had an assignment to write about what I wanted to be when I grew up. Since it's obvious that I didn't end up singing bass for The Pointer Sisters, I had to find a new calling.

After being on the graphics side of things in several startups and working closely with the devs, I found myself more and more drawn to the idea of being on the building side of what I had been designing.

What I wouldn't give to have seen my teacher's face when she was grading that paper...
